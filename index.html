Hi!

I'm Gautham Vasan, currently an Artificial Intelligence (AI) Engineer at Kindred Systems Inc.. We're working towards building machines with human-like intelligence. My research interests include: Reinforcement Learning, Deep Learning, Robotics, Human Cognition and Artificial Intelligence.

I recently graduated with a M.Sc in Computing Science from the University of Alberta. I worked under the supervision of Dr. Patrick M. Pilarski as a part of the BLINC Lab and the Reinforcement Learning and Artificial Intelligence (RLAI) Lab headed by Dr. Richard S. Sutton.

As a part of my masters, I worked predominantly with rehabilitative and assistive robots. My thesis research was on Learning from Demonstration: Teaching a Myo-electric Prosthesis with an intact limb via Reinforcement Learning . This work has also been nominated for the "M.Sc. Outstanding Thesis Award".

I graduated from National Institute of Technology (NIT), Tiruchirappalli with a Bachelor's degree in Instrumentation and Control Engineering in 2015, working with Dr. A. Ramakalyan and Dr. V. Sankaranarayanan. During that time, I had a fruitful collaboration with Dr. Saravana Ilango for the Texas Instruments India Innovation Challenge - 2014. In the past, I've also worked with Dr. K. Madhava Krishna at the Robotics Research Center, IIIT Hyderabad.

Deep Reinforcement Learning for Hex

Hex is a strategy board game played on a hexagonal grid, theoretically of any size and several possible shapes. The model is a convolutional neural network, trained with a variant of Q-learning, whose input is player positions and edge connections and whose output is a value function estimating future rewards. This work was presented at the IJCAI Computer Games Workshop 2016, New York, USA. Paper
